Financing Solutions For Your Firm.

Law firms practicing in the area of personal injury law face unique business challenges. Typically attorneys are compensated through contingency fees so they are only paid once a lawsuit settles. Since lawsuit timeframes can take months or years, attorneys practicing in this area have to plan on delayed compensation. At the same time they incur ongoing expenses to operate their business including payroll and office leases. Moreover, often these attorneys face the additional obligations of supporting substantial litigation expenses including discovery efforts and hiring experts.

Serving the Need of our Attorney Base

Alliance believes that a small practitioner from time to time may have short term cash needs. Securing traditional financing from a traditional banking institution can take weeks or even months not to mention 3 years of audited Financials on both your business and personal life. At Alliance we can quickly evaluate your case load and provide financing up to 100,000+ within a 48 Hours' time period.

Rising Cost of Litigation

With the rising cost of litigation many law firms are faced with not expanding their scope of practice and continuing to grow for only handling a smaller case load and in some cases even outsourcing cases only to receive a referral fee. With firms often having to pay hundreds of thousands of dollars in out if pocket expenses this is simply just not a good business practice. Alliance Legal Solutions offers several attorney financing products that can fill the gap in the traditional lending market a way that helps law firms survive cash crunches and grow their businesses.

Products

Pending Contingency-Fee Cases

Alliance finances your expected attorney fees on pending contingent fee cases. The process is simple, you provide Alliance with information on one or more of your contingent fee pending cases and Alliance provides financing based on your expected fees.

Settled Cases

If you have settled your client's legal claim and are waiting to receive the proceeds, Alliance will purchase a portion of your fee at a discount to the full value of the fee. This is similar to factoring on business receivables; however Alliance use your legal fee as the receivable asset.

Appellate Cases

If you have received a judgment for your client that is currently being appealed, Alliance Legal Solutions can advance you a portion of the anticipated legal fee.

Earned Hourly Fees

In certain cases Alliance will offer financing on a portion of your earned but not yet collected hourly fees. Typically the types of fees we will finance include those for representation of insurance companies or work that is paid by a government entity.
